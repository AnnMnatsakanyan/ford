1965 and 2021 Mustang Fastback

Unveiled on April 17, 1964 at the Worldâs Fair in New York, the Mustang Fastback changed the way we see sports cars. It was so fresh, fast and completely one-of-a-kind that they had to create the pony car segment just to categorize it. With the classic long deck and short hood that remains today, the original Mustang looked as fast as it felt.

1969 and 2021 Mustang Mach 1

Named after the speed of sound, the 1969 Mustang Mach 1 brought Mustang into a new wave of performance and customization. Standard-issue in the Mach 1 was the two-barrel H-code 351 Windsor, which offered 250 horsepower. A complete package, the Mach 1 came with a hood blackout, scoop, side and trunk stripe decals, quad exhaust tips and chrome-styled steel wheels. And, for added comfort on the track, Ford equipped this marvel with competition suspension and a unique deluxe interior with wood-grained trim.
